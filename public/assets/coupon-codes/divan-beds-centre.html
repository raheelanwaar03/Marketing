About Divan Beds Centre
Go to
https://www.divancentre.co.uk/

There are many reasons to choose a divan base and mattress, and divans have become popular around the UK, because of their low cost and also because of their storage capabilities.

Being an online company we save on administrative costs and overheads and we are pleased to be able to pass these savings on to our customers. When you buy through us you can be sure you are making some great savings without compromising on quality, and with our exceptional customer service we are with you every step of the way.

All prices on the website are final. VAT is included in the price and we also have free delivery. There are no hidden charges.

All of our divan bases have the option of drawers for storing anything from towels and bed linen to clothes and books. This is particularly worthwhile when space in the home is at a premium as the large, spacious drawers can really make a difference in providing additional storage. We sell the full single or double divan package, or if you prefer you can choose a divan base only or indeed just the mattress.

As well as solid divan bases or platform bases, we also have divan beds with spring bases. These have a layer of springs that cover the top of the base to provide a softer feel.

In addition to divan beds, we also provide a wide range of other bed frames, pillows and mattresses, including traditional metal framed beds and wooden framed beds, plus more unconventional leather beds. We offer plenty of choice in mattresses, including mattresses for zip and link beds. This type of mattress is perfect for a spare room as it provides the flexibility to have either twin beds or a double bed. Zip and link beds are particularly popular in hotels and B+B's for this reason.

All of our beds and mattresses are brand new and manufactured in the UK, complying with all UK flammability regulations. Our commercial range of products all comply with the BS7177 fire retardant standard, making them ideal for hotels and other establishments that require a higher standard of flame resistance. Since we have been in business we have managed to build up a solid reputation and we believe it is our high quality designs and focus on customer service that has helped us achieve this.

We also make custom sized beds for customers who need something a particular type of bed that is not usually available.
