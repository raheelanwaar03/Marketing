About First Furniture
Go to

The Company

The First Furniture team is made up of people with extensive experience of furniture manufacturing and retailing, marketing, IT, finance and logistics. We have over 28 years of experience in the furniture industry and also a huge wealth of experience in retail customer service gained through highly successful businesses in Scotland. Our buyers travel the world sourcing new and exciting design-led furniture giving you an unparalleled choice of beautiful pieces at the best possible prices. Together we recognized that there is a better way for people to shop for furniture rather than travelling from furniture showroom to furniture showroom hunting for the right product at the right price.

At First Furniture, we want your shopping experience to be as enjoyable and as easy as possible. We want to give you all the information you need throughout the order process, and because we care about our customers and the service they expect, you can rely on us to keep you informed every step of the way. You can shop from the comfort of your home and away from all the pressures of salesman´s of your typical furniture showroom.

From placement of your order, we are constantly monitoring its progress, and our quick and reliable Customer Service Team are always on hand to answer any questions you might have along the way. In essence, we offer our customer a huge collection of quality furniture at exceptionally low prices. We offer great customer service, great delivery service, and extremely quick delivery. Our lead-times for furniture are remarkably shorter than your typical furniture showroom as we maintain a high level of stock of our most popular ranges in our massive warehouses around the country.

First Furniture T/A Morale Home Furnishings UK Ltd is part of the Morale UK Group of Companies.

Our friendly and experienced staff are also available to answer any question you might have. We hope you enjoy the First Furniture experience!,
