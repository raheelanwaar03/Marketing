About WJD Exclusives

We meet and exceed our customers needs through quality products at reasonable prices with exceptional service.

WJD Exclusives established in 2002.

It all started with a dream. A dream of crafting the highest quality jewelry and providing it to loyal customers at extremely affordable prices. After years of planning and developing, WJD Exclusives was established in 2002, in New York, NY. Since 2002, we have relied on 3 simple principles:

  • Exceptional quality.
  • Lowest price.
  • Fanatical service.
    (not necessarily in that order)

Despite over 15 years of growth and change, our business philosophy and love for jewelry have remained consistent. We now sell nationwide and in some commerce platforms worldwide. Our team and customers have grown with us and stuck with us through thick and thin, just as families do. Our 3 principles drive our business approach and we cannot wait to share them with you.

Why should you shop with us?

WJD Exclusives treats every single customer like family. Our aim has always been to provide highest quality jewelry at the lowest possible price. We offer a wide selection of engagement rings, wedding bands, bridal sets, pendants, necklaces, earrings, studs, fashion jewelry, certified diamonds, and more! Browse our site, pick what you like, choose from our easy financing options (ask our customer rep for details) and enjoy your time shopping for jewelry with us.

Along with our finely crafted jewelry, we also offer in-house financing (ask our customer rep for details), free jewelry gift box, diamond upgrades, and much more! Experience the WJD Exclusives difference from the comfort of your home.
